Cheetah Bae

A Cheetah can run up to 75 MPH for 400m!

  • Scientific Name: Acinonyx jubatus
  • Average Length: Up to 59 inches and 160 lbs
  • Average Lifespan: 10 to 15 years
  • Habitat: Out in the wild somewhere aka The Savannah

Cheetahs will walk you down in the wild! They will run you down so fast you will literally look like you were walking!
